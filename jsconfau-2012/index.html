Performance is the priority

  • Aesthetics are secondary to percieved performance.
  • Consider: box-shadow, gradients, even rounded corners might incur a render penalty.
  • You can avoid this if you position:absolute everything (or transform:matrix3d() ...everything).
  • Many vectors for failure: network latency, offline, templating vs markup.
